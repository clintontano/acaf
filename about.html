About ACAF

Promoting intellectual liberty and defending the rights of scholars across the continent.

Our Mission

To act as a continental watchdog and advocate for the protection of academic freedom, ensuring that higher education institutions in Africa remain spaces of free inquiry, critical thinking, and innovation.

Our Vision

An Africa where scholars, researchers, and students can pursue knowledge without fear of persecution, censorship, or political interference.

Our Core Values

⚖️

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in our research, advocacy, and operations.

🤝

Solidarity

We stand united with scholars facing threats, regardless of their location or discipline.

🌍

Inclusivity

We champion diverse voices and perspectives within the academic community.

Promoting academic freedom and institutional autonomy across the African continent.
