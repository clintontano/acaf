Who We Are

Safeguarding the Future of African Education

Academic freedom is the cornerstone of a vibrant, innovative, and democratic society. Across the continent, scholars and institutions face increasing pressures that threaten their ability to teach, research, and speak freely.

ACAF stands as a guardian of these fundamental rights, uniting educators, researchers, and policymakers to build resilient academic ecosystems.

Our Strategic Pillars

We approach the defense of academic freedom through four interconnected pillars of action.

👁️

Monitoring

Tracking and documenting violations of academic freedom to ensure accountability and transparency.

📢

Advocacy

Engaging with governments and international bodies to shape policies that protect scholars.

📚

Research

Producing data-driven reports and analyses on the state of higher education in Africa.

🤝

Capacity Building

Empowering institutions and individuals with the tools to defend their rights.
