Who We Are

Safeguarding the Future of African Education and Democracy

Academic freedom is the cornerstone of a vibrant, innovative, and democratic society. Yet, across the continent, institutions, scholars and students face increasing pressures that threaten their ability to teach, research, produce/disseminate knowledge, speak truth to power and challenge orthodoxy.

ACAF stands as a guardian of these fundamental principles, uniting educators, researchers, students, policymakers and civil society to build resilient academic ecosystems.

Our Strategic Pillars

We approach the defense of academic freedom through four interconnected pillars of action.

👁️

Monitoring

Tracking and documenting violations of academic freedom to ensure accountability and transparency.

📢

Advocacy

Engaging with governments and international bodies to shape policies that protect scholars.

📚

Research

Producing data-driven reports and analyses on the state of higher education in Africa.

🤝

Capacity Building

Empowering institutions and individuals with the tools to defend their rights.
