Our Focus Areas

We implement a multi-faceted approach to safeguard academic rights.

Monitoring & Documentation

We systematically track attacks on academic freedom across the continent. This includes documenting specific incidents of harassment, imprisonment, and censorship.

  • Regional monitoring networks
  • Annual "State of Freedom" reports
  • Rapid response alerts

Advocacy & Policy

We engage with national governments, the African Union, and university administrations to adopt policies that protect academic freedom.

  • Policy brief development
  • Legal defense funds
  • Strategic litigation support

Research & Knowledge Production

ACAF conducts rigorous research to understand the structural causes of academic freedom violations and to propose evidence-based solutions.

Our research serves as a vital resource for journalists, policymakers, and human rights defenders.
