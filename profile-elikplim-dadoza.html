Elikplim Dadoza

Administrative Staff

Elikplim Dadoza is a pragmatic development practitioner with a proven track record in working with the private sector and on donor-funded social intervention projects. Experienced professional with a comprehensive skill set in project management, monitoring and evaluation, and entrepreneurship training within the framework of international development initiatives.

She has successfully led projects spanning education, digital skills, entrepreneurship, monitoring and evaluation, and social impact across diverse cultural and organizational contexts. Beyond technical expertise in project management, Elikplim has a strong background in entrepreneurship training, having empowered individuals and businesses to thrive by delivering tailored training sessions and mentorship programs.

Her collaborative and pragmatic approach has enabled her to work effectively with diverse stakeholders, including donor agencies, private sector actors, and community-based organizations. A passionate advocate for sustainable development and social impact, Elikplim is committed to driving positive change through evidence-based solutions.

She holds a Master's degree in Development Studies and various certifications in project management and impact evaluation.
