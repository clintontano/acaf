Conrad Kakraba

Communications Consultant

Conrad Kakraba is a Communication and Media Consultant with extensive experience in journalism, academia, and strategic public affairs.

He worked as a Newscaster, Editor, and Reporter at Ghana Television (GTV) and was the Founding Executive Director of Truth TV. He served as Director of Media and Public Affairs and a Communication Lecturer at Heritage Christian University. He also taught Communication at GIMPA Business School and the Faculty of Law.

In leadership and advocacy, he served as National President and Communication and Public Relations Manager for United Nations Association of Ghana.

He holds an MA in Journalism and Mass Communication from Sikkim Manipal University (now Academic City University) and a BA in English with Linguistics from University of Ghana.

His work spans media leadership, communication strategy, teaching, and public engagement, with a strong commitment to professional excellence and national development.
